## Albert Pike — Morals and Dogma (1871)

- **Title:** Morals and Dogma of the Ancient and Accepted Scottish Rite of Freemasonry
- **Author:** Albert Pike, Sovereign Grand Commander of the Southern Jurisdiction of the Scottish Rite
- **Date:** 1871
- **Pages:** 861
- **Language:** English
- **Access:** 🟢 Public domain. Multiple editions on Internet Archive.
- **Significance:** Foundational Scottish Rite text covering all 32 degrees. Contains the "Lucifer, the Light-Bearer" passage (19th degree chapter) that generated the entire anti-Masonic "Lucifer-worship" accusation chain (Pike → Taxil → modern conspiracy literature). Draws extensively on Kabbalistic sources throughout.
