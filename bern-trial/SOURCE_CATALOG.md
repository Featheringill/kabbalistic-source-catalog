# Bern Trial (1934–1937) — Source Catalog

The Bern Trial was the only judicial proceeding in history to formally examine the authenticity of the Protocols of the Elders of Zion. The 1935 verdict found them to be a forgery; the 1937 appeal reversed the conviction on procedural grounds while leaving the forgery finding intact.

## Statutory Basis

The prosecution was brought under the Canton of Bern's 1916 law on Schundliteratur (trashy/harmful literature), Arts. 14–16.
