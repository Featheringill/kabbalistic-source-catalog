# Sha'ar ha-Gilgulim — Source Catalog

## Primary Source
- **Title:** שער הגלגולים (Sha'ar ha-Gilgulim / The Gate of Reincarnations)
- **Author:** Rabbi Chayyim Vital (1543–1620), recording the teachings of Rabbi Isaac Luria (1534–1572)
- **Edition used:** Frankfurt 1684
- **Digital source:** [hebrewbooks.org/43534](https://hebrewbooks.org/43534)
- **Copyright status:** Public domain

## About the Work
The Sha'ar ha-Gilgulim is the most comprehensive Jewish text on the transmigration of souls (gilgul). It addresses the five soul-levels (Nefesh, Ruach, Neshamah, Chayyah, Yechidah), the mechanics of transmigration, ibbur (positive soul-attachment), and the specific gilgul-histories of biblical and rabbinic figures.

## Structure (40 Chapters across 3 volumes)

| Vol | Chapters | Scope |
|-----|----------|-------|
| 1 | 1–14 | Foundational doctrine: the five soul-levels, conditions for gilgul |
| 2 | 15–27 | Mechanics: what binds a soul to return, ibbur, sin-and-repair categories |
| 3 | 28–40 | Specific gilgul-histories: Adam through the Safed masters; eschatological resolution |
